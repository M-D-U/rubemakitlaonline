About Rubben Makitla Pty Ltd

Delivering sustainable industrial solutions through integrity, innovation, and long-term partnerships.

Who We Are

At Rubben Makitla Pty Ltd, our culture is built on collaboration, innovation, and sustainability. We empower our team to contribute ideas that improve safety, productivity, and environmental responsibility.

Our client-focused approach ensures tailored, high-quality solutions across dust suppression, logistics, lubricants, and machinery services.

  • Eco-conscious operations
  • Trusted long-term partnerships
  • Expertise in challenging environments

Our Journey

Founded in 2019, Rubben Makitla Pty Ltd was established to address the impact of dust, logistics inefficiencies, and inadequate lubrication on industrial productivity and safety.

Through innovation and industry expertise, we developed solutions that improve operational efficiency while protecting the environment.

Today, we continue to grow as a trusted partner delivering sustainable, high-performance services across South Africa.
