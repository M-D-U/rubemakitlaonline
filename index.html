About Rubben Makitla Pty Ltd

Founded in 2019, Rubben Makitla Pty Ltd was established to address critical challenges in dust control, transportation efficiency, and equipment maintenance within the mining, construction, and agricultural sectors. Through innovation, sustainability, and industry expertise, we have grown into a trusted partner delivering tailored, high-quality solutions.
